### 🚨 Red Flags with Free VPNs

1. **They sell your data** — If it is completely free with no limits, you are the product
2. **No privacy policy** — Run away
3. **Unknown company** — Stick to established names
4. **Requires too many permissions** — Be suspicious

### Our Pick

Proton VPN offers unlimited free data with a strict no-logs policy. The only catch: free servers are medium speed and limited to 3 countries.

### Why Would a VPN Be Free?

Some VPNs use free tiers as marketing — hoping you upgrade to paid. These are safe (Proton, Windscribe). Others sell your browsing data to advertisers. Avoid those.
