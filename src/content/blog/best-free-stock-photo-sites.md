### #1 Unsplash — Best Overall

The largest collection of high-quality free photos. Strong search with collections curated by designers. API available for integrations.

### #2 Pexels — Best for Video

Photos and videos, both free. Video quality is surprisingly good for free content. Search works well in multiple languages.

### #3 Pixabay — Best Variety

Photos, illustrations, vectors, videos, and sound effects — all free. Largest collection overall but quality varies more than Unsplash.
