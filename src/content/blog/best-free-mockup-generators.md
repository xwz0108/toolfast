### #1 Shots.so — Best for SaaS

Create beautiful browser mockups for your web app. Multiple angles, backgrounds, and browser chrome styles. Modern and clean.

### #2 Placeit — Best Variety

Largest collection of mockup templates. Free tier has watermarks but enough to prototype. Paid plans are affordable for the volume.

### #3 Mockuuups — Best for Apps

Drag and drop your screenshots onto device frames. Supports iPhone, Android, MacBook, and desktop mockups. Free tier includes 10 templates.
